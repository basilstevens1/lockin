Frequently Asked Questions

How does LockIn work?

You set a timer for a task. During that time, LockIn prevents you from leaving the app or accessing distracting sites. You're locked in until the timer ends.

Can I cancel a session?

No — that's the point! Once you start, you're committed. Emergencies? We'll have a limited override option, but it's designed to be tough.
